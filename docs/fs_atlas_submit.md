Want to share your Maps4FS creation with the community? You can submit your map to **FS Atlas** and make it available for other players to enjoy!

## How to Submit

The submission process is currently being refined. For now:

1. **Join the Discord Community**: Connect with the Maps4FS community on [Discord](https://discord.gg/Sj5QKKyE42)
2. **Contact Me on Discord**: Reach out to discuss publishing your map
3. **Provide Map Details**: Share information about your map (location, features, screenshots, etc.)

üìù **Note**: Detailed submission documentation will be added as the platform develops. In the meantime, Discord is the best way to get your map published.

## What You Can Include

When submitting your map, you can provide:

- **Attribution**: Your name or username as the map creator
- **Links**: Direct links to your social media, website, or other platforms
- **Donation Platforms**: Links to your Patreon, Ko-fi, PayPal, or other donation/support platforms
- **Description**: Information about your map's features, location, and gameplay characteristics

All creator attributions and links will be preserved and displayed alongside your map on FS Atlas.

## Benefits of Submitting

- **Reach a Wider Audience**: Share your creation with the Farming Simulator community
- **Get Feedback**: Receive comments and suggestions from other players
- **Support Your Work**: Include donation links so players can support your map-making efforts
- **Build Your Portfolio**: Showcase your Maps4FS creations in one place

## Questions?

If you have questions about submitting your map or the submission process, please reach out on Discord.
